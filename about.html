I started as a freelancer and spent years in the trenches of video editing. Along the way I noticed something that most people in this industry overlook: not every editor understands the platform they're editing for.

Great content is not just about clean cuts or smooth transitions. It is about understanding what triggers a viewer to stop scrolling, watch to the end, and take action. That takes knowledge of social media trends, audience psychology, and platform algorithms — not just editing software.

That gap is exactly why I built EditorsUnited. A one-stop solution for content creators who want their videos to actually work. Every project we take on combines scripting, idea crafting, and strategy — so your content is built to perform from the very first frame. No hustle, no guesswork.
