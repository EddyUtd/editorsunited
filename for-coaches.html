Common questions

What coaches ask before they sign up.

I don't want to be "that guy" dancing on TikTok. Can you still help?

Yes — our edits lean expertise-first, not performative. Think educational hooks, reframed insights, case-study breakdowns. Authority content, not trend-chasing.

What do I need to record?

Short talking-head clips from your phone — 2-5 minutes per video idea, batched weekly. We handle scripting, hooks, b-roll, captions, music, and CTA. You record; we turn it into 7–30 finished edits.

How fast do I see booked calls?

Most coaches see their first DMs within 30 days and booked discovery calls within 60. Consistency compounds — the clients who commit to 3+ months see the biggest lifts.

What if I don't know my offer well enough yet?

Part of our onboarding is an offer audit — we help you clarify your angle before we start editing. If your offer is too vague, your content will be too. We fix that together.
