Common questions

Will my edits still sound like *me*?

Yes — the onboarding call is specifically to lock in your voice: your pacing, your captions, your on-camera style. Our goal is edits your audience can't tell weren't done by you at 2am.

I already have an editor. Why switch?

Most editors cut videos. We engineer them. That means hook research, retention pacing, CTA framing — the strategic layer most freelancers skip. If your current edits feel flat, that's the gap.

How much raw footage do I need to record?

Depending on the package: 1-2 hours of batched phone recording per week yields 7-30 finished edits. We coach you on what to record after the strategy call.

What if I'm not posting consistently yet?

Perfect timing. The whole point of our service is to remove the consistency bottleneck. We'll help you batch, script, and build a schedule you can actually keep.
