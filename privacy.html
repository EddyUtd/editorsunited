Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

Last updated: April 2025. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your Instagram handle or business name
  • Your content goals and any message you provide

We do not collect payment information directly. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

2. How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and discuss your content needs
  • Recommend the right package for your goals
  • Communicate with you about your project

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Portfolio and marketing use

By working with EditorsUnited, you agree that we may use videos, reels, and content we have edited for you as examples of our work. This includes use on our website, social media profiles, and in promotional materials. We will never identify you without your permission if you request anonymity. If you do not want your content featured, let us know in writing before the project begins.

4. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website:

  • Cloudflare — website hosting, DNS, security, and basic analytics
  • Web3Forms — form submission processing and email delivery
  • Stripe — secure payment processing for subscriptions

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses Cloudflare's infrastructure, which may set basic security cookies. We use Cloudflare analytics to understand general traffic patterns. We do not use advertising cookies or track individual users across other websites.

6. Data retention

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and manage any ongoing project relationship. If you would like your information removed from our records, contact us at support@editorsunited.com and we will action your request promptly.

7. Contact us about privacy

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, reach out directly at support@editorsunited.com. We will respond within 48 hours.
